Hello! Welcome to my webpage.

I am a PhD candidate in the Logic and Theory Group at the University of Bern, working under the supervision of [Thomas Studer](https://home.inf.unibe.ch/thomas.studer/).
My research lies in Non-wellfounded Proof Theory, with broader interests in Modal Logic, Provability Logics and Arithmetical Theories.
I am particularly interested in the mathematical study of the foundations of mathematics.

Before the PhD, I did the [Master of Logic](https://msclogic.illc.uva.nl/) at the ILLC in the University of Amsterdam.
If you want to have a glimpse to my thesis it is available [here](https://msclogic.illc.uva.nl/theses/recent/publication/5372/On-the-Provability-Logic-of-Constructive-Arithmetic-The-%CE%A3_1-provability-logics-of-fragments-of-Heyting-Arithmetic).
